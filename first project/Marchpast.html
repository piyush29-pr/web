Marchpast

St. Michael Higher Secondary School in Satna showcased its discipline, precision, and school spirit through an impressive March Past event. The marching display was a testament to the school's commitment to instilling values of order, coordination, and unity among its students. The March Past, a visual spectacle held at the school grounds, featured neatly aligned contingents from different classes and houses. The participants, adorned in their respective school uniforms, moved in perfect unison, reflecting the result of rigorous training and practice.
